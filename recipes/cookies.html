Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper for taste
  • Scallions
Items you need
  • pan
  • bowl
  • spoon
  • knife
  1. Crack eggs inside of a bowl
  2. Grab the salt, and pepper, and pour a few milligrams inside of the bowl.
  3. Grab the spoon, and mix all the ingredients together.
  4. Use your knife to cut a fourth piece of butter off.
  5. Place the butter inside of the pan, and turn the pan on as well.
  6. Place Eggs mixture inside of the pan, and cook for a few minutes until the eggs solidify a little.
  7. Use your clean spoon and flake the egg.
